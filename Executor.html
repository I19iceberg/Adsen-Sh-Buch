Executor Appointment

I appoint my son, Can Paul, as the executor of this will. In the event that he is unable or unwilling to serve, I appoint my daughter, Elif Paul, as the alternate executor.

Executor Powers

I grant my executor full power and authority to manage and dispose of all my properties and assets as described in this will. This includes the power to sell, transfer, or otherwise dispose of any property or asset, whether real or personal, tangible or intangible, wherever located. The executor is not required to obtain court approval for any actions taken in their capacity as executor.
